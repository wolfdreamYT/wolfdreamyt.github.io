Webbot has been discontinued

WolfStudios has officially discontinued WebBot, a platform where users can extract article information for free and automatically formatted for viewers preference.

This decision has been made due to modern company standards and legal reasons. This means WebBot is no longer supported and servers have been shutdown indefinately.

Any users or clients in possession of WebBot are advised that WolfStudios is no longer responsible for continued use, modifications, or support. Further, warranty, or support obligations are nullified effective from the date of this announcement. Users are strongly encouraged to uninstall WebBot and seek alternative solutions.

The effective date of this announcement is 2025-12-22 at 8:30 PM (or 20:30 in military time). We will not accept EES (Extended Enterprise Support). Thankyou for understanding and apologies for the inconvenience.
